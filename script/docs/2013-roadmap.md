# 2013 CRM Roadmap

This project roadmap captures the major milestones for the WorkSuite CRM initiative from September to December 2013.

- September 2013: initial platform foundation and architecture planning.
- October 2013: sales pipeline, lead capture, and follow-up workflows.
- November 2013: invoicing automation, reporting, and dashboard views.
- December 2013: team collaboration, release hardening, and launch readiness.
